Latitude vs. Wind Speed


For the scatter plot of City Latitude vs Wind Speed shows that both north and south of the equator there is a consistent wind speed between 0-10 mph. There are several outliers farther away from the equator which may coincide with wind patterns in those areas resulting in stronger winds than usual. Winds are typically stronger at higher elevations and open spaces. Most of the cities randomly pulled may have been from areas that do not match those criteria.
